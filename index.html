Devslopes was created to offer a human alternative to apathetic coding bootcamps and archaic universities. We identified the number one reason why aspiring software engineers can’t land a job: They don’t have any professional experience. Our mentors teach you how to land paid projects that you can use to build an incredible resume of real experience. But that's not all! Hackathons are a major part of our community: Our students pair up in teams to compete against one another to build real world projects that we pay them for. And this year alone, we've paid out over $25,000 to our Hackathon winners!
